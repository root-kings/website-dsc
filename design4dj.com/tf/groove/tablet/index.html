MORE INFO

  • GENEREAL

    Entrance

    This year we have two different entrances, remember the entrance when you enter the club, because this is also the exit where you will leave the club. At each exit it's stated where that exit leads to (public transport shuttles and / or different parkingplaces).

     

    Come prepared

    Bring your sunglasses and some clothes for in the evening, you can store them in the lockers. Pressurized cans and perfume bottles are not permitted. Deodorant and sunscreen are only allowed in the original sealed container, but are also available at the toilets inside the festival.

     

  • RULES

    • Bring your happy face!

    • Age 18 + (don't forget your ID)

    • Always follow the instructions of our staff, security, parking and emergency services personnel

    • No fireworks, weapons and sharp or dangerous objects

    • Soft drugs like hash & weed are not allowed, Security will thoroughly search everybody and will collect all drugs

    • If hard-drugs is found you will be removed from the festival and might be handed over to the police

    • It's not allowed to bring food / beverage / drinks

    • Camera surveillance is part of our security program for your and our safety

    • No (professional) photo / film / video / audio equipment (standard digital-cameras / phones allowed)

    • No (Dutch) football shirts or shirts with offending lyrics/symbols etc. No shirts off!

    • No smoking on the dancefloor. Smoking on the outdoor stages is allowed
